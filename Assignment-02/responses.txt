Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.

Data travels in the internet by being placed in packets those packets are then sent to their intended destination from one machine
to another.  After which they are unpacked and reassembled to their original form.

What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

IP addresses are the address assigned to your computer to identify it on any given network or connection.
Opposingly a DNS address is a Domain Name Service and it lets you access files on a server or particular domain

What is a cookie?

Cookies are a method by which websites can save information about and identify users.
For example cookies can save things like your preferred language so that a website can use that language whenever you are on it.
